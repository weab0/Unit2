Education required to become an animator

You dont need one!

In order to become an animator, all you really need is a drawing tablet, a computer, and art skills. But a degree does help when apply for animation jobs.A college degree will help teach you tips and tricks of animation and will overall help your animation become more prefesional. But in order to get an animation job,a degree isnt required.
